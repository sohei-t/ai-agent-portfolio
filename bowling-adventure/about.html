Game Overview

Bowling Adventure reimagines the classic bowling experience with a modern twist. Instead of a simple straight lane, you'll navigate through an exciting obstacle course filled with sand traps, icy surfaces, and challenging terrain.

Using your device's tilt sensors or a virtual joystick, guide your ball through each course to reach the pins. Master the physics, find the perfect line, and aim for that perfect strike!

Complete a full 10-frame game with authentic bowling scoring, including strikes, spares, and bonus throws in the final frame.
